CURATORSHIPS

This space is dedicated to authorial curatorships made with artworks from the Homeostasis Lab platform. A space for reflection on the impacts of digital media on the poetry, practice and aesthetics of contemporary art.

Homeostasis Lab - New Time brings together exhibitions signed by curators Flávio Carvalho, Gabriel Menotti, Guilherme Teixeira, Luciana de Paula Santos, Mari Nagem, Paloma Oliveira, Pedro Caetano, Rudá Cabral and Taís Koshino
